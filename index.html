Did you know?

There are over 10,000 known species of birds worldwide, ranging from tiny hummingbirds to massive ostriches.

Some birds undertake incredibly long migrations. For example, the Arctic Tern flies from the Arctic to the Antarctic and back each year.

The peregrine falcon is the fastest bird and the fastest animal on Earth. It can reach speeds of up to 240 miles per hour when diving to catch prey.

Lyrebirds of Australia are known for their exceptional ability to mimic natural and artificial sounds, including chainsaws and camera shutters.
